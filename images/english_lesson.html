3️⃣ Simple Present Tense

Use the Simple Present to talk about habits, routines, and things that happen regularly.

📐 Grammar Structure

✅ Affirmative Examples

Subject + Base Verb + Complement

• I wake up at 6 a.m.
• I brush my teeth every morning.
• I take a shower before work.
• I drink coffee in the morning.
• I go to work by car.
• I work from Monday to Friday.
• I study English every week.
• I watch TV at night.
• I cook dinner for my family.
• I sleep at 10 p.m.

❌ Negative Form

Subject + do/does + NOT + Base Verb + Complement

• I do not (don't) wake up early.
• I do not drink coffee.
• I do not watch TV every day.
• He does not (doesn't) work on weekends.

❓ Questions

Do/Does + Subject + Base Verb + Complement?

• Do you wake up early?
• Do you drink coffee?
• Do you work every day?
• Do you study English?
• Do you cook at home?
• Does he work on weekends?

💬 Short Answers

• Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
• Yes, he does. / No, he doesn't.

4️⃣ Simple Past Tense

Use the Simple Past to talk about completed actions that happened at a specific time in the past.

📐 Grammar Structure

✅ Affirmative Examples

Subject + Past Verb + Complement

• I woke up at 6 a.m. yesterday.
• I brushed my teeth after breakfast.
• I took a shower before work.
• I drank coffee in the morning.
• I went to work by car.
• I worked all day.
• I studied English yesterday.
• I watched TV last night.
• I cooked dinner yesterday.
• I slept at 11 p.m.

❌ Negative Form

Subject + did + NOT + Base Verb + Complement

• I did not (didn't) wake up early.
• I didn't drink coffee.
• I didn't work yesterday.
• She didn't cook dinner last night.

❓ Questions

Did + Subject + Base Verb + Complement?

• Did you wake up early yesterday?
• Did you drink coffee yesterday?
• Did you work yesterday?
• Did you study English yesterday?
• Did you cook dinner yesterday?
• Did she go to work by car?

💬 Short Answers

• Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.
• Yes, she did. / No, she didn't.

5️⃣ Conversation Practice - Today (Simple Present)

Practice speaking about your daily routine using the Simple Present tense.

👤 Student A:
What time do you wake up?
👥 Student B:
I wake up at ________.
👤 Student A:
Do you drink coffee?
👥 Student B:
Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
👤 Student A:
Do you work or study?
👥 Student B:
I work as a ________.
OR
I study ________.
👤 Student A:
What do you do at night?
👥 Student B:
I watch TV.
I study English.
I cook dinner.
I sleep early.

📝 Homework Assignment

Complete these assignments to reinforce what you've learned in class.

Task 1: Write 5 sentences about your daily routine

Use the Simple Present tense to describe what you do every day.

Example:
  1. I wake up at 6 a.m.
  2. I brush my teeth.
  3. I drink coffee.
  4. I go to work.
  5. I sleep at 10 p.m.

Task 2: Write 5 sentences about what you did yesterday

Use the Simple Past tense to describe your activities from yesterday.

Example:
  1. I woke up at 6 a.m.
  2. I brushed my teeth.
  3. I drank coffee.
  4. I worked all day.
  5. I watched TV at night.

Task 3: Interview a classmate

Ask your classmate at least 5 questions about their daily routine and their activities yesterday. Write down their answers.

Questions to ask:
  1. What time do you wake up?
  2. What do you do in the morning?
  3. Do you work or study?
  4. What did you do yesterday?
  5. What did you do last night?
