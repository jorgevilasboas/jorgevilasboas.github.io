Family Members & Relationships

⏱️ Duration: 2 Hours
📚 Level: Beginner
🎯 Focus: Vocabulary & Possessives

1️⃣ Family Vocabulary

Learn how to talk about your family members and relatives.

Immediate Family

  • Father (Dad)
  • Mother (Mom)
  • Parents
  • Brother
  • Sister
  • Siblings

Extended Family

  • Grandfather (Grandpa)
  • Grandmother (Grandma)
  • Uncle / Aunt
  • Cousin
  • Nephew / Niece

Relationships

  • Husband / Wife
  • Son / Daughter
  • Children
  • Grandson / Granddaughter

2️⃣ Verbs for Family Life

Essential verbs to describe family interactions and history.

• I love my family.
• We live in a big house.
• I visit my grandparents on weekends.
• He helps his brother with homework.
• They married ten years ago.
• I grew up in a small town.

3️⃣ Grammar: Possessives

Possessive Adjectives

SubjectPossessiveExample
IMyMy father is a doctor.
YouYourIs that your sister?
HeHisHis brother is tall.
SheHerHer name is Mary.
ItItsThe dog likes its toy.
WeOurOur family is happy.
TheyTheirTheir house is blue.

Saxon Genitive ('s)

Use 's to show possession for people.

• John's mother (A mãe de John)
• My sister's car (O carro da minha irmã)
• My parents' house (A casa dos meus pais - *Note: only ' for plural ending in s*)
