3. SIMPLE PRESENT
==================================================

Structure:

Subject + Verb + Complement

Examples:

• I wake up at 6 a.m.
• I brush my teeth every morning.
• I take a shower before work.
• I drink coffee in the morning.
• I go to work by car.
• I work from Monday to Friday.
• I study English every week.
• I watch TV at night.
• I cook dinner for my family.
• I sleep at 10 p.m.

Negative Form:

• I do not (don't) wake up early.
• I do not drink coffee.
• I do not watch TV every day.

Questions:

• Do you wake up early?
• Do you drink coffee?
• Do you work every day?
• Do you study English?
• Do you cook at home?

Short Answers:

• Yes, I do.
• No, I don't.

==================================================
4. SIMPLE PAST
==================================================

Structure:

Subject + Past Verb + Complement

Examples:

• I woke up at 6 a.m. yesterday.
• I brushed my teeth after breakfast.
• I took a shower before work.
• I drank coffee in the morning.
• I went to work by car.
• I worked all day.
• I studied English yesterday.
• I watched TV last night.
• I cooked dinner yesterday.
• I slept at 11 p.m.

Negative Form:

• I did not (didn't) wake up early.
• I didn't drink coffee.
• I didn't work yesterday.

Questions:

• Did you wake up early yesterday?
• Did you drink coffee yesterday?
• Did you work yesterday?
• Did you study English yesterday?
• Did you cook dinner yesterday?

Short Answers:

• Yes, I did.
• No, I didn't.

5. CONVERSATION PRACTICE – TODAY
==================================================

Student A:

What time do you wake up?

Student B:

I wake up at ________.

Student A:

Do you drink coffee?

Student B:

Yes, I do. / No, I don't.

Student A:

Do you work or study?

Student B:

I work as a ________.
OR
I study ________.

Student A:

What do you do at night?

Student B:

I watch TV.
I study English.
I cook dinner.
I sleep early.
